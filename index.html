About

Actor Abhinav Choudhary, who rose to fame for playing the role of Rajkumar Nakul in &TV;’s Paramavatar Shri Krishna was born on 29 JAN 1992 in Begusarai,Bihar.

He did his schooling from GD College, Begusarai. He did his graduation in B.com Accounts hons from College of commerce, Patna.
He did his post-graduation in MBA-IMED from Bharati Vidyapeeth, Pune.
He did his Diploma from Roshan taneja acting school & started acting in Parichay theatre.
His acting career started from Parmavtar Shri Krishna (Mahabharat ka Arambh) where he was playing Rajkumar Nakul.
